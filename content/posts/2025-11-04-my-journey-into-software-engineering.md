# From Classroom to Code: My Transition Story

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I'm excited to share my journey from being a mathematics teacher to pursuing a career in software engineering.

## The Decision to Change

After 10+ years in education, I found myself drawn to the logical problem-solving aspects of programming. Teaching mathematics had already given me a strong foundation in analytical thinking, and I realized that coding was a natural extension of those skills.

## Why Software Engineering?

- **Problem-solving**: Just like in math, programming is about breaking down complex problems into manageable pieces
- **Creativity**: Building applications allows me to create tools that can help people
- **Continuous learning**: The tech industry's constant evolution matches my love for learning
- **Impact**: Technology has the power to reach and help more people than I could in a single classroom

## What I'm Learning

Through LaunchCode's program, I'm diving deep into:

- **Frontend Development**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React
- **Backend Development**: Java and Spring Boot
- **Database Management**: SQL Server
- **Version Control**: Git and GitHub
- **Problem-solving methodologies**: Applying my mathematical background to coding challenges
